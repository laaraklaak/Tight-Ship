⚓ Tight Ship - Financial Command CenterTight Ship is a private, offline-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for freelancers, independent contractors, or anyone managing unpredictable income alongside strict recurring expenses.Instead of overwhelming you with charts or connecting directly to your bank account, Tight Ship acts as a manual, intentional ledger. It tells you exactly what you have, exactly what you owe, and exactly what is safe to spend today.✨ Core FeaturesTrue "Safe to Spend" Calculator: Instantly subtracts your unpaid bills from your cash on hand, so you never accidentally spend money that is already spoken for.Outstanding Bills Queue: A dedicated holding area for bills that have arrived but haven't been paid yet. Mark them as "Paid" with one tap when your income arrives.Zero Backend & Offline First: Your financial data stays on your device using LocalStorage. The app works flawlessly without an internet connection.Private Cloud Backup: Securely syncs your ledger to a hidden appDataFolder in your personal Google Drive. The app creator (and Google) cannot see your data.Mobile-Optimized Dark Mode: Built with Tailwind CSS and Phosphor Icons for a premium, app-like experience on iOS and Android.Receipt Capture: Snap photos of your receipts and save them directly to the app (compressed locally on your device).🚀 Getting StartedTo run your own instance of Tight Ship, you only need a static web host (like GitHub Pages) and a Google Cloud Client ID for the backup feature.1. Clone & HostClone or fork this repository.Host the files using GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, or any static hosting provider.2. Enable Google Drive Sync (Optional but Recommended)To allow the app to back up data to Google Drive, you must generate a Google Client ID:Go to the Google Cloud Console.Create a new project and enable the Google Drive API.Configure the OAuth Consent Screen (set to "External" and add your email as a Test User).Go to Credentials, click Create Credentials -> OAuth Client ID (Web Application).Add your hosting URL (e.g., https://yourusername.github.io) to the Authorized JavaScript origins.Copy your generated Client ID.3. Connect the IDOpen index.html, scroll down to the JavaScript <script> tag, and replace YOUR_GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID with your actual ID:// index.html (Line ~377)
const GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID = '123456789-abcdefg.apps.googleusercontent.com'; 
🛠️ Tech StackFrontend: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, Tailwind CSS (via CDN)Icons: Phosphor IconsStorage: Browser localStorageCloud Sync: Google Identity Services & Google Drive REST API v3PWA: Standard manifest.json and Service Worker (sw.js)📱 Installing on Your PhoneBecause this is a PWA, you don't need an App Store to install it:iOS (Safari): Open your hosted link, tap the "Share" icon at the bottom, and select "Add to Home Screen".Android (Chrome): Open your hosted link, tap the three dots menu, and select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen".Run a tight ship, stay afloat.
