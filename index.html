Welcome to Tight Ship

Your simple personal finance tracker. Log what comes in, what goes out, and what's still owed — and always know exactly where you stand.

Income — money received

Spend — money spent

Bill — recurring or upcoming payments

Your Dashboard

At a glance, the dashboard shows three things:

Available Balance

Income minus all actual spend and paid bills. This is what you have right now.

Income & Spend totals

Side-by-side so you can see the flow at a glance.

Unpaid Bills

Money you still owe. Does not affect your balance until you mark it paid.

Logging a Transaction

Tap Log in the nav bar. Choose a type, fill in the amount and details, then save.

Income

Enter the source (e.g. "Salary") and the date received. This adds to your balance immediately.

Spend

Enter the vendor and date. Deducted from your balance straight away.

Bill

Toggle whether it's already paid. If paid, it deducts now. If unpaid, set a due date — it sits in Unpaid Bills without touching your balance.

Managing Bills

Unpaid bills appear at the top of your Ledger. Note: Your ledger is collapsed by default! Tap "Ledger" to expand it.
